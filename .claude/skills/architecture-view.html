10. BA — BRD Validation Gate & Knowledge Bank NEW

Format-agnostic validation: extracts concrete behaviours from any BRD format. Knowledge bank pre-answers anticipated questions. No concern silently dropped.

flowchart TB
    BRD["Read brd-raw.md\n(any format)"] --> Q{"Can you extract\nat least ONE concrete\nexpected behaviour?"}

    Q -->|"NO — zero behaviours"| HARD_BLOCK["HARD BLOCKER\nEscalate to human:\nDescribe what should happen"]
    HARD_BLOCK --> HUMAN_INPUT["Human provides\nbehaviours"] --> BRD

    Q -->|"YES — behaviours found"| COUNT["Report count\n+ note gaps"]

    COUNT --> GAPS{"Other context\nthin?"}
    GAPS -->|"no why, no scope, etc."| NOTE["Note gaps as concerns\nfor Q&A — do NOT block"]
    GAPS -->|sufficient| PROCEED["Proceed to Step 2"]
    NOTE --> PROCEED

    PROCEED --> EXTRACT_OQ["Extract BRD's own\nopen questions / TBDs"]
    EXTRACT_OQ --> STEP2["Step 2: Internal Analysis\n3-lens concern collection"]

    STEP2 --> KB_READ["Read knowledge-bank.md"]
    KB_READ --> EACH_CONCERN{"For each concern"}
