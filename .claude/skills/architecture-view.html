4. Architect-Analyst-ProductEx Verification Cycle

3-cycle max loop. Analyst pushes back on security flaws, perf issues, breaking changes, AND unfulfilled product requirements (via ProductEx).

flowchart TB
    ARCH["Architect produces\n01-architect.md"] --> U1{"User\napproves?"}
    U1 -->|continue| STAGE1

    subgraph ANALYST_WORK["Analyst Phase 02"]
        STAGE1["Stage 1: Own Impact Analysis\nblast radius, security, perf, breaking changes"]
        STAGE1 --> GRD["Check GUARDRAILS.md\nG-01, G-03, G-07"]
        GRD --> STAGE2["Stage 2: Spawn\nProductEx verify"]
    end

    STAGE2 --> VERDICT{"ProductEx\nverdict?"}

    VERDICT -->|approved| OK["Analyst returns\ncomplete + verification table"]
    VERDICT -->|changes needed| BLOCK["Analyst raises BLOCKER\nCategories:\n1. Impact Analysis Issues\n2. Product Requirements Issues\nwith evidence + direction"]

    OK --> SHOW_OK["Show user:\nfindings + approval"] --> NEXT["Proceed to Designer"]

    BLOCK --> SHOW["Show user: Cycle N/3\nAll issues with evidence\nSuggested directions"]

    SHOW --> CK{"Cycle\ncount?"}

    CK -->|"< 3"| APPROVE{"User approves\nrework?"}
    APPROVE -->|continue| RERUN["Architect re-runs\nwith blocker evidence"]
    APPROVE -->|resolve| MANUAL["Human takes\nmanual control"]
    RERUN --> ARCH

    CK -->|"= 3"| ESC["HUMAN ESCALATION\n3 cycles exhausted"]
    ESC --> OPT["Options:\nA. Manual adjust architect\nB. Accept with risks\nC. Revisit BA requirements\nD. Consult product team"]
    OPT --> DECIDE["Human decides"] --> CONT["Continue workflow"]

    CHK -->|">= 2"| CB["CIRCUIT BREAKER\nEscalate to Human\n4 options"]
    CHK -->|"< 2"| SEV{"Orchestrator\nclassifies severity"}

    SEV -->|trivial| TRV["Auto re-run Phase N\nwith blocker appended"]
